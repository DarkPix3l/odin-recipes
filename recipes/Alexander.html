Granpa's Brandy Alexander

I got this recipe from my Italian Granpa, who used to make it for me at Christmas time. Perfect for a choco-holic like me. A guaranteed winner! Serve in any glass and garnish with whipped cream and sprinkle on chocolate powder.

  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Preparation time: 5 min
  • Cooking time: 5 min
  • Serves: 2
  • Cost: Low

INGREDIENTS

SERVING 2

  • 4 tablespoons brandy
  • 4 tablespoons creme de cacao
  • 4 tablespoons chocolate syrup
  • 2 cups chocolate ice cream, softened
