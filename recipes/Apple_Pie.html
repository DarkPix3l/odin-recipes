Apple Pie by Grandma Ople

This incredible apple pie recipe comes from my grandmother. I've never encountered another recipe quite like it! It will always be my favorite and has even won several first-place prizes in local competitions. Perfect as a dessert for family dinners or during the holidays, it's guaranteed to impress.

  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Preparation time: 30 min
  • Cooking time: 1 hr
  • Serves: 8 people
  • Cost: Low

INGREDIENTS

FOR ONE (9-inch) PIE

  • 8 small Granny Smith apples, or as needed
  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup packed brown sugar
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 (9-inch) double-crust pie pastry, thawed
