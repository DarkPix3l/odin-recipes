Artichoke lasagna Recipe

Artichoke lasagna is a rich and appetizing dish, a vegetarian alternative to classic baked lasagna, prepared with the traditional and timeless Bolognese sauce. The cheeses and a velvety bechamel will guarantee a succulent stringy effect, while the artichokes, with their sweet and bitter taste, will give their typical and unmistakable flavour. This special white lasagna with winter vegetables will thus be enriched with new and inviting aromas and scents. Next Sunday change the menu and bring artichoke lasagna to the table, a seasonal first course also ideal for Easter lunch!

  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Preparation time: 20 min
  • Cooking time: 45 min
  • Doses for: 6 people
  • Cost: Low

INGREDIENTS

FOR A 20X30 CM PROFILE

  • Dry egg lasagne 250g
  • Scamorza (provola) 400g
  • Grana Padano DOP to grate 250g
  • Extra virgin olive oil 4 tablespoons
  • Salt - as needed
  • Artichokes 12
  • Taleggio 200g
  • White wine 1 glass
  • Shallot 1
  • Black pepper as needed

FOR THE BECHAMELLA

  • Whole milk 1l
  • Butter 100g
  • Salt - as needed
  • Flour 00 Type 80g
  • nutmeg - a dash
